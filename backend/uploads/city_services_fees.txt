SMART CITY MUNICIPAL SERVICES - FEE SCHEDULE

BIRTH CERTIFICATE FEES
- Birth Certificate (within 1 year of birth): Rs. 50
- Birth Certificate (after 1 year): Rs. 100
- Duplicate Birth Certificate: Rs. 150
- Urgent/Tatkal Birth Certificate: Rs. 300
- Birth Certificate correction: Rs. 200

HOW TO APPLY FOR BIRTH CERTIFICATE
1. Visit the Municipal Corporation office or apply online at the city portal.
2. Submit the hospital discharge summary or doctor's certificate.
3. Provide parents' identity proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID).
4. Pay the applicable fee at the counter or online.
5. Certificate will be issued within 7 working days.
6. Tatkal certificates are issued within 2 working days.

DEATH CERTIFICATE FEES
- Death Certificate (within 1 year): Rs. 50
- Death Certificate (after 1 year): Rs. 100
- Duplicate Death Certificate: Rs. 150

MARRIAGE CERTIFICATE FEES
- Marriage Certificate registration: Rs. 100
- Duplicate Marriage Certificate: Rs. 200

PROPERTY TAX
- Residential property tax: 0.5% of property value per year
- Commercial property tax: 1% of property value per year
- Late payment penalty: 2% per month

WATER CONNECTION FEES
- New domestic water connection: Rs. 2000
- New commercial water connection: Rs. 5000
- Water connection transfer: Rs. 500

TRADE LICENSE FEES
- Small business (up to 500 sq ft): Rs. 1000 per year
- Medium business (500-2000 sq ft): Rs. 3000 per year
- Large business (above 2000 sq ft): Rs. 5000 per year

WASTE MANAGEMENT
- Residential waste collection: Free (included in property tax)
- Commercial waste collection: Rs. 500 per month
- Bulk waste disposal: Rs. 200 per trip
